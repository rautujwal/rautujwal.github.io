Insurance Policy Prioritisation

The problem

A portfolio of 1.05M insurance records with no structured way to prioritise which policies were worth investigating first. Review effort was spread equally across everything — expensive and ineffective.

The approach

Built an 8-step SQL workflow that worked through baseline benchmarking, lift scoring, Pareto concentration analysis, tenure cohort comparisons and segment stability checks — systematically ruling things out until the real concentration revealed itself at the policy level.

02
Python · Pricing Review

Used Vehicle Pricing Review Tool

The problem

The used car market is volatile by nature. Price depreciation depends on mileage, age, fuel type, transmission and more — making it genuinely difficult to know what a fair price looks like. Most buyers and sellers have no reliable way to check before committing to a deal.

The approach

Built a pricing decision support tool across 8,128 used vehicle listings. Users enter their vehicle details and the tool returns an expected market price, benchmarks their price against comparable listings, and delivers a plain-language pricing summary — without needing to interpret numbers themselves. A simulation layer lets buyers and sellers adjust their price and instantly see how market positioning changes, before going to market.

  • Vehicle feature input and data preparation
  • Expected price estimation from market comparables
  • Price positioning assessment relative to similar listings
  • Interactive price simulation layer
  • Plain-language pricing summary
  • Dashboard output for distribution and key factors
